Why this matters

You can't think
your way to clarity.

The body responds before the mind catches up. When you're under pressure, activated, or in protection mode — the quality of everything you think, say, and decide changes. Most people never notice.

Biometrics without meaning

Wearables give you numbers. But nothing connects those numbers to how you're actually showing up — in a conversation, a decision, a moment that matters.

Reactivity you don't see coming

Most poor decisions happen when the body is under strain — not because of bad judgment, but because we're in protection mode and don't realise it.

Resilience that doesn't stick

Meditation builds habits. Therapy builds insight. Neither gives you a live signal in the moment when your capacity to stay present is actually being tested.

Regulate mode

Work with what's present

Something's in the way — procrastination, overwhelm, a difficult conversation. Or something's here to be felt — grief, joy, longing. Name it, locate it in the body, and work with it through breathwork and inquiry.

  • Emotion and challenge check-in
  • Body location and intensity mapping
  • Breathwork calibrated to your state
  • Guided reflection and insight
Discern mode — for leaders

Lead from clarity, not reactivity

Before a high-stakes decision or a difficult conversation, check in with your body first. Know whether you're deciding from a clear place — or from pressure, fear, or protection mode.

  • Body check-in before a decision
  • Polarity mapping for ongoing tensions
  • Decision journal with outcome tracking
  • Live wearable context (Oura, Apple Watch, Whoop)
Embody mode

Move what's stuck

Every emotion is a movement pattern in the body. When something needs discharging — or you want to train access to a state you need — Embody gives you a guided movement practice built around what's present.

  • Emotion-matched movement sequences
  • Express (discharge) or Access (cultivate) modes
  • Live heart rate feedback during movement
  • Post-session body readout
How it works

Not another
mindfulness timer.

We use your wearable's data — HRV as context, heart rate as a live signal — to show you what's happening in your body right now. The practice happens in the moment when it counts.

01

Something shows up

A tightness, a heaviness, a decision looming. Your wearable notices a shift — or you do. Either way, you open the app at the moment it actually matters.

02

Name what's present

What's in the way, or what's here to be felt? Where do you feel it? The app translates body sensation into language — without needing you to have it figured out first.

03

Work with it, not against it

Breathwork, inquiry, or a body check-in before a decision — depending on what you need. No generic sessions. The practice is built around what your body just reported.

04

Build the picture over time

Sessions accumulate into a picture of your patterns — what activates you, what grounds you, and whether your body has been a reliable guide to your decisions.
