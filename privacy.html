Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

Regulate ("we", "our", "us") helps people stay grounded under pressure by combining guided breathwork, body-based practices, and real‑time biometric context from wearable devices. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Regulate is operated by Philip Brady, trading as Regulate, based in the United Kingdom. For the purposes of UK and EU data protection law, Philip Brady is the "data controller" of the information described below.

Information we collect

Depending on how you interact with us — joining the waitlist today, or using the Regulate app once it launches — we may collect:

  • Waitlist information: your email address, your stated area of interest (e.g. leadership, coaching, personal regulation), and, optionally, the wearable device you use.
  • Account information: name, email address, and any profile details you choose to provide when you create an account in the app.
  • Biometric and health data: heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), and related signals sourced from connected wearables such as Oura Ring, Apple Watch, and Whoop, used to provide real-time context during Regulate, Discern, and Embody sessions.
  • Usage data: information about how you use the app, such as session type, duration, and in-app interactions, so we can improve the product.
  • Device information: basic technical data (device type, operating system version) needed to operate the app reliably.

How we use your information

  • To operate and provide the core features of Regulate, including biometric-informed breathwork, decision check-ins, and movement practices.
  • To send waitlist updates, launch announcements, and, if you opt in, product communications.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the app and troubleshoot issues.
  • To understand aggregate usage patterns so we can prioritise what to build next.

We do not sell your personal data or biometric data to third parties, and we do not use your health data for advertising purposes. We do not use advertising identifiers or cross-app/website tracking, and Regulate does not serve third-party ads.

Legal basis for processing (UK/EU users)

Where UK or EU data protection law applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — for biometric/health data from wearables, and for marketing emails. You can withdraw consent at any time, as described under "Your choices and rights" below.
  • Contract — to create your account and provide the core app features you've signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the waitlist and app secure, prevent misuse, and understand aggregate usage, balanced against your rights and interests.
  • Legal obligation — where we must retain or disclose information to comply with the law.

Sharing your information

We share data only as needed to run the service:

  • Wearable and health platforms (e.g. Apple Health, Oura, Whoop) — only to sync the biometric data you explicitly connect and authorise.
  • Service providers who host our infrastructure or send communications on our behalf, bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.
  • Legal reasons — if required to comply with the law, protect our rights, or ensure user safety.

We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

International data transfers

We may store or process information using service providers located outside your home country, including in the United States and United Kingdom. Where this involves transferring personal data out of the UK or EU, we use appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses or the UK International Data Transfer Addendum) to protect it.

Data storage and security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

Retention: we keep waitlist information until the waitlist closes or you ask us to delete it, whichever is sooner. Once the app is live, we keep account and biometric data for as long as your account is active, and for a limited period afterward to allow you to reactivate it, unless you ask us to delete it sooner or the law requires a different period.

Your choices and rights

  • You can unsubscribe from waitlist or marketing emails at any time via the link in those emails.
  • You can disconnect a wearable integration at any time, which stops new biometric data from being collected.
  • You can request access to, correction of, deletion of, or a copy of your personal data, or object to or restrict certain processing, by contacting us below.
  • If you're in the UK or EU and unhappy with how we've handled your data, you also have the right to complain to your local data protection authority (in the UK, the Information Commissioner's Office).

Children's privacy

Regulate is not directed at children, and it is not intended for use by anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll update the "Last updated" date above, and where changes are material we'll provide additional notice.
