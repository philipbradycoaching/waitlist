Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Regulate ("we", "us", or "our") is built by Philipp Brady. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data we collect

Regulate may collect the following:

  • Health and biometric data — heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), and respiratory rate, read from Apple HealthKit or a connected wearable (Oura, Apple Watch, Whoop). This data is used solely to personalise your in-app experience.
  • Session data — your check-in responses, emotion selections, body sensations, and reflections. This is stored locally on your device.
  • Waitlist data — if you signed up via our waitlist, we hold your name and email address.

How we use your data

  • To show you relevant biometric context during sessions
  • To build your personal journal and pattern history
  • To improve the app based on aggregate, anonymised usage
  • To contact you about early access (waitlist only)

Health data and HealthKit

Regulate uses Apple HealthKit to read heart rate and HRV data. We do not write data to HealthKit. Health data is never shared with third parties, used for advertising, or sold. It is not uploaded to any server — all processing happens on your device.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except where required by law.

Data storage

Session and journal data is stored locally on your device. Waitlist contact details are stored securely via Formspree. We do not operate our own servers that hold your health or session data.

Your rights

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in your device settings. To have your waitlist data removed, email us at the address below.

Children

Regulate is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
